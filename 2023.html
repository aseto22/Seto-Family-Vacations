2023

In 2023, we went to many places and experienced new and fun things.


New Year's - Half Moon Bay

For New Year's, we went to Half Moon Bay. It's my first time staying up all the way to midnight!



Winter Break - LA

For winter break/ski week, we went to LA. We visited some colleges, Dodger Stadium, the Warner Bro Studio, and family.



Spring Break - Safari West

For spring break, we went to Safari West! We saw so many cool animals. The giraffes and bearded monkeys were so cute!



Summer Vacation - Oregon

One of the biggest trips of the year was our roadtrip to Oregon. It was a ton of fun. We saw Crater Lake which was beautiful, Shasta Dam, the Harry and David factory, University of Oregon, and Oregon State University.



Samantha Graduation Trip + Beach

In the summer, we went down to LA for Samantha's high school graduation. We also went to the beach.



Great Wolf Lodge

In October we went to Great Wolf Lodge! It's an indoor water park. We also stayed at their hotel. The flower petal tube ride was incredible!



Thanksgiving Break - Las Vegas

For Thanksgiving break, we went to Las Vegas! We saw a ton of things including the Sphere, the Strat, the Las Vegas Sign, the Neon Museum, Red Rock Canyon, Zion National Park, and Mat Franco the magician. We stayed at the Luxor.



Holiday Break - LA and Mexico

For Holiday break, we went to Los Angeles! We celebrated Christmas with our cousins and family. We also went on a 3 day Carnival cruise to Ensenada, Mexico with our cousins and family.
