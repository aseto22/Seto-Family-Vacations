2017

In 2017, we went to many places and experienced new and fun things.


Winter Break - San Diego and Disneyland

For winter break/ski week, we went to San Diego! We saw the San Diego Zoo, San Diego Safari Park, and Disneyland! All the animals were incredible!



Spring Break - Las Vegas and Grand Canyon

For spring break, we went to Nevada and Arizona! We went to Las Vegas! It was our first time there. We went to buffets, many casinos, and smelled a lot of smoke. On the way to the Grand Canyon, we stopped at Hoover Dam. We also were right on the Nevada, Arizona border. The Grand Canyon was beautiful! So wide and deep.



Memorial Day Weekend - Watsonville

For the long weekend, we went to Watsonville! We hiked Elkhorn Slough, picked strawberries, and went to Gizdich Ranch for their ice cream and apple dumplings. It was so good and fun!



Summer - Texas

For summer vacation, we went to Texas! It was our first time in Texas. We saw Mommy's old house, our second counsins, George W. Bush Museum (We saw Laura Bush here!), Perot Museum, the Reunion Tower, and AT&T Stadium!



Summer - LA

During summer break, we went to LA! We played with our cousins!



Holiday Break - Florida, Disney World, Disney Cruise, and LA

For Holiday break, we went to Florida and LA! In Florida we went to Disney World and rode all the fantastic rides. We also went on a Disney Cruise near the Bahamas. It was our first ever cruise as a family. All of it was super fun! Finally, we flew to LA for Christmas and celebrated it with our family and cousins.
