2022

In 2022, we went to many places and experienced new and fun things.


Winter Break - Sacromento

For winter break/ski week, we went to Sacromento. We went to a Quarry Park, which is like a ropes course in an old quarry mine. We also saw the Jelly Belly Factory!



Spring Break - Yosemite

For spring break, we went to Yosemite! The nature was so cool and beautiful! It was our first time at this national park.



Summer Vacation - Pismo Beach

One of the biggest trips of the year was going to Pismo Beach. It was a ton of fun at the beach.



Summer - Roseville

In the summer, we went to Roseville! We kayaked here!



Holiday Break - Los Angeles

For Holiday break, we went to LA!
